About Me

I am a fifth-year PhD Student in the Augmented Perception Lab at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, and in the HCI Lab at the Interactive Technologies Institute at Instituto Superior Técnico - University of Lisbon, advised by Prof. David Lindlbauer and Prof. Augusto Esteves. I am part of the CMU Portugal Dual Degree PhD Program in Human-Computer Interaction at CMU and IST. My research explores how to improve Extended Reality (XR) training and collaboration, through leveraging different mechanisms to maintain awareness between people, within oneself, and in interaction with intelligent agents. I am primarily interested in XR, Training and Collaboration, and 3D User Interfaces.

I received my Integrated MSc. degree in Aerospace Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico - University of Lisbon in 2020.

I am currently based in Lisbon!
