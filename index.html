About Me

I am a second-year PhD Student in the INESC_ID Research Center at Instituto Superior Técnico / University of Lisbon and in the Augmented Perception Lab at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Joaquim Jorge, Prof. David Lindlbauer and Dr. Maurício Sousa. I am part of the CMU Portugal Dual Degree PhD Program in Human-Computer Interaction at CMU and IST. I am excited about research in Remote Collaboration, Perception Manipulation, Interaction Techniques, 3D User Interfaces, and Mixed Reality.

I received my Integrated MSc. degree in Aerospace Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico / University of Lisbon in 2020.

I am currently based in Pittsburgh!


Personal Curiosities

Design and Fabrication Portfolio

Project 1| DIY Cup Holder

❮ ❯

The GreenGlam paper cup holder enables you to hold your hot beverage while protecting your hand from heat. Features an ergonomic handle molded specially to a human hand, and a mini-holder for milk, half-half, or other extras you want to add to your hot beverage. Made from acrylic material though laser cut process and heatgun mold. GreenGlam’s cup holder is perfect for the environmentally conscious hipster who values both style and substance!

Download .rdl for laser cutting.

Project 2| Transforming Takeout

❮ ❯

The Cupcake CardStand is a delightful way to present your singular cupcake. This unique and innovative cardbox is not only functional as a container to store your cupcake, but it also transforms into a beautiful small cupcake stand that can be used to showcase your delicious creation. It is perfect for gifting or any occasion where a single cupcake needs to stand out.
